🎓 AIOU Student Support Services

Assistance at Every Step of Your Academic Journey

Allama Iqbal Open University (AIOU) is committed to providing comprehensive student support services to ensure learners succeed. Whether it’s about admissions, academic counseling, or technical help, AIOU ensures support is always available.

📌 Types of Student Support Services

📝 Admission Assistance

AIOU offers a transparent admission process. In case of confusion, students can contact the admission office or visit any of the regional centers across Pakistan for help.

📚 Academic Counseling

Students receive guidance in selecting suitable programs and planning their academic paths. Counselors also help with study strategies, exam prep, and time management.

💻 Technical Support

For LMS (AAGHI Portal) issues like login errors or assignment uploads, AIOU has a technical team ready to assist via helpline or email.

👩‍🏫 Tutoring Services

Students interact with tutors via LMS for help with course materials, assignments, and exams. Tutors also provide feedback to improve academic performance.

🧾 Examination & Results Assistance

Support includes exam schedules, roll number slips, and results access. Students can also track their performance on the portal and receive grading updates.

💼 Career Counseling

Counselors assist with resume writing, job searching, and planning professional careers based on qualifications.

☎️ Helpline & Regional Centers

AIOU helpline and regional offices are available for student queries, whether related to enrollment or academics—offering both online and face-to-face support.

✅ Conclusion

From admissions to graduation, AIOU’s Student Support Services ensure students receive timely help and guidance. Whether you need technical, academic, or career support—AIOU stands with you every step of the way.
